# Node Types

Use these node types in the LLM Wiki.

## concept
An idea, term, principle, theory, or course concept.

Examples:
- A key term in your field
- A principle or theory
- A technical concept you reference often

## person
A person relevant to work, school, projects, or personal context.

## project
A defined ongoing effort.

Examples:
- A degree or course of study
- A content/brand project
- A side business

## system
A recurring process or operating system.

Examples:
- [[Calendar Packet Workflow]]
- A recurring tracking process you run

## formula
A mathematical or operational formula.

Examples:
- Compound Interest
- Break-Even Point
- A domain formula you use regularly

## workflow
A repeatable sequence of steps.

Examples:
- Calendar Commit Workflow
- Daily Review
- Write-Up Process

## decision
A durable choice that affects future behavior.
