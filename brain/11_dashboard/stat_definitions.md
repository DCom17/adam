# Stat Definitions

Eight stats track development across key life areas.
Each stat uses the same XP curve and level scale.
Progression requires real evidence — not intentions or plans.

---

## Quest Eligibility Rule

An action qualifies as a daily quest only if it passes at least one of these two gates:

**Stat gate:** The action meets the qualifying evidence criteria for at least one of the 8 stats below.
**Boss gate:** The action directly advances a confirmed milestone for an active boss.

Actions that fail both gates are tasks only — they do not earn XP and should not receive quest IDs.

Always fails (examples):
- One-off errands (ordering supplies, picking something up)
- Passive scheduled time with no intentional investment
- Planning or intention without execution
- Admin tasks with no stat or boss linkage
- Passive entertainment, scrolling, routine co-presence

Use the "Evidence examples" and "Does NOT count" sections below to apply the [[XP System|stat gate]].

---

## 1. Discipline

What it measures: consistency, follow-through, and self-regulation.

Evidence examples:
- Completing a planned routine (morning, evening, work block)
- Executing a scheduled task without avoidance or delay
- Maintaining a habit for multiple consecutive days
- Following through on a commitment under resistance or fatigue

Does NOT count: planning without execution, talking about discipline, starting but not finishing

Anti-fake rule: only counts when effort was governed, timely, and intentionally controlled. Last-minute panic relabeled as "discipline" does not qualify.

Primary linked bosses: [[Build Daily Discipline System]] (direct), all others (supporting)

---

## 2. Knowledge

What it measures: structured learning, comprehension, and applied understanding.

Evidence examples:
- Completing a course section, chapter, or exam
- Reviewing and retaining study material with notes or recall
- Reading technical or professional content with demonstrated understanding
- Passing assessments or quizzes

Does NOT count: skimming, passive reading without retention, watching content without engagement, endless videos, link hoarding, note beautification without recall

Anti-fake rule: reward retrieval and application — recall drills, practice tests, teach-back, syntheses, passing assessments. If there is no recall, testing, explanation, or application, default to 0.

Primary linked bosses: your education and career bosses

---

## 3. Health

What it measures: physical capability, fitness habits, and body maintenance.

Evidence examples:
- Completing a workout session
- Playing pickleball or another physical activity
- Meeting a sleep target (logged or confirmed)
- Intentional nutrition choices confirmed in daily log

Does NOT count: planning to work out, passive rest, mild walking as the only activity, buying gear, reading about fitness

Anti-fake rule: reward consistency and objective markers (workouts, sleep protection, recovery, appointments, nutrition execution), not gear purchases or one heroic session after a long drought.

Primary linked bosses: [[Improve Health/Fitness]]

---

## 4. Finance

What it measures: financial stability, tracking, and intentional money management.

Evidence examples:
- Setting up an automatic transfer or financial automation
- Reviewing spending or budget
- Paying off or reducing debt
- Increasing savings
- Completing a financial task (canceling subscription, setting up account, etc.)

Does NOT count: thinking about money, intentions without action, passive awareness, repeated balance checking, speculative "research"

Anti-fake rule: Finance XP reflects control, resilience, and choice — budgeting, paying, transferring, reducing debt, systemizing. Rechecking balances twelve times is anxiety, not progress.

Primary linked bosses: [[Stabilize Finances]]

---

## 5. Career

What it measures: professional growth, skill development, and career progress.

Evidence examples:
- Work shift completed with strong performance
- Applying career skills or professional knowledge on the job
- Working on a content/brand or other career-building project
- Completing a professional development action with a concrete output

Does NOT count: idle career thinking, vague "working on it," passive job browsing, endless resume tweaking, generic career-content consumption

Anti-fake rule: Career XP requires outbound action, shipped work, or objective proof of increased market value — applications sent, portfolio pieces produced, capability demonstrated.

Primary linked bosses: your career and brand-building bosses

---

## 6. Spiritual

What it measures: reflection, values alignment, clarity, and inner stability.

Evidence examples:
- Intentional reflection (journaling, prayer, meditation)
- Making a decision clearly aligned with stated values
- Expressing gratitude intentionally
- Taking deliberate time to reset, not just passive entertainment

Does NOT count: passive downtime, entertainment, avoidance labeled as "rest", performative logging, ritual-count inflation

Anti-fake rule: internal practice counts, but it must be sincere and lived. Logging ritual counts without actual practice, repair, service, or alignment defaults to low or zero XP.

Primary linked bosses: none directly — supports all others

---

## 7. Social

What it measures: quality of relationships and commitment to others.

Evidence examples:
- Honoring a commitment to family, GF, or friends (Madoka with brother, gardening with GF)
- Meaningful time with another person — present, not just nearby
- Reaching out or following up with someone intentionally
- Maintaining a relationship through active investment

Does NOT count: passive co-presence, defaulting to plans that were already scheduled, scrolling, shallow reactions, networking theater

Anti-fake rule: Social XP must reflect relationship quality, presence, repair, or contribution — not proximity without real investment.

Primary linked bosses: none directly — Social health supports all bosses

---

## 8. Execution

What it measures: completing tasks and shipping work — not just starting.

Evidence examples:
- Closing a task from `05_tasks/active_tasks.md` with confirmed completion
- Finishing a project deliverable
- Closing an open loop that was previously unresolved
- Following through from start to full completion on a meaningful task

Does NOT count: starting tasks, partial work without closure, moving things to "in progress", endless system tweaking, organizing work instead of doing it

Anti-fake rule: shipping, submitting, finishing, clearing real blockers. System maintenance only counts if it removes a real blocker or produces a tool actually used in live work.

Primary linked bosses: your execution and discipline bosses, and all others (supporting)
