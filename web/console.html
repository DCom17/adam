Make it an app (add to home screen)
Once Adam is open on your phone, add it to your home screen so it gets its own icon and opens full-screen like a normal app:
  • iPhone (Safari): tap the Share button (square with an up-arrow) → Add to Home Screen → Add.
  • Android (Chrome): tap the ⋮ menu → Add to Home screen / Install app.
The first time you open it from the home-screen icon, it may ask for your access token again — the installed app has its own private storage. To sign it in: turn on Cautious mode above, tap Show token QR, and scan that code with your phone Camera — it'll offer to Copy the token. Then paste it into the app's token box (your Adam URL is usually already filled in) and you're in for good.
